What is Covid-19

COVID-19 is a global pandemic and has caused millions of deaths worldwide.

Origin

COVID-19 originated in bats and spread to humans through contact with an intermediate animal host, most likely in the Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market in Wuhan, China. There is no direct evidence to support the theory that COVID-19 escaped from a laboratory.

Transmission

It can spread from person to person through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes. It can also spread through contact with contaminated surfaces or objects.

Symptoms

COVID-19 can cause a wide range of symptoms, including fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, and muscle aches. Severe cases can lead to pneumonia, acute respiratory distress syndrome, and death./p>

Tips

The best way to protect yourself from COVID-19 is to get vaccinated and boosted, wear a mask in public indoor settings, practice social distancing, and wash your hands frequently.
